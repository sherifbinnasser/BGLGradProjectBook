\noindent
{\textbf{\Huge Abstract}}
\vspace{1cm}

This project aims to design and develop a smart wheelchair with advanced control mechanisms to enhance mobility and independence for individuals with physical disabilities. 
\newline

The smart wheelchair integrates multiple advanced control mechanisms, including joystick operation, face recognition, and eye tracking technology, to accommodate users with varying levels of mobility and dexterity.
\newline

The joystick control provides a familiar and intuitive interface for users who retain upper body mobility. For users with limited hand functionality, the face recognition control mode allows for navigation through facial movements and expressions. Furthermore, the eye tracking system offers an innovative solution for users with severe physical limitations, enabling wheelchair control through eye movements. By leveraging state-of-the-art technologies in computer vision and human-computer interaction, the smart wheelchair ensures a versatile and user-friendly interface.
\newline

Additionally, the wheelchair is integrated with a Healthcare System that monitors and records sensor measurements related to the user's health. This data can be used for ongoing health assessments and to provide real-time feedback to healthcare providers.
\newline

The goal of this project is to create an adaptable, reliable, and safe mobility solution that significantly improves the quality of life for individuals with mobility challenges.
